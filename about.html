About Us

Reliable Fastening Solutions Saving Time and Money

Yueda provides a very wide and comprehensive products range. We are one of the few factories in China and Southeast Asia with automatic production lines manufacturing staples, brads and collated nails.

Shanghai Yueda Nails Co., Ltd. was established in 2003. The company headquarter is located in Shanghai. Yueda has 3 manufacturing plants in Shanghai, Anhui Chuzhou and Southeast Asia. Now Yueda has construction area of 88,000 square meters.

The company has ERP management system and ISO9001 quality control system to ensure the whole process of production control and product quality. The company has passed ISO9001 certification, ISO14001 certification, obtained CE, ICC, ETA and other international certifications, and passed the BSCI.
